About Me

Independent photographer working in documentary and street photography, gives lectures on the history of photography, has lived all her life in Lviv, Ukraine. Member of the National Union of Photographers of Ukraine.

Focused on projects documenting the effects of war in a rear city, personal stories, and routine work supporting the Armed Forces of Ukraine. Also focuses on revealing elements invisible at first glance, finding beauty in very simple things, the beauty of the unnoticed.
