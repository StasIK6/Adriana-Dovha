About Me

Sashko

This is a small visual story about courage and gratitude for the gift of living and surviving, about strength and fragility, about light and pain, about the vastness of God's plans and the intertwining of human paths…

No one knows how God chooses heroes, how a young man at 24 goes to defend his land from the Russian invasion, and how a year later he becomes an example of strength of spirit, because while rescuing a comrade from the battlefield, he receives a severe injury, losing three limbs. Instead of despair, a new stage begins – overcoming oneself. He has only one hand, but a huge heart, a quick mind eager to learn, many new purposes, a circle of friends, a new job, and grand plans for the future… all this is about Oleksandr Bazylevych, an incredibly strong, positive, and bright military veteran.

Sashko was born in Konotop, Sumy region. He graduated as an ecologist from Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv in 2021. At the end of February 2022, he arrived in Lviv and began independently studying explosives, drones, weapons, and tactical medicine, attended several classes in the Territorial Defense of Lviv, and on January 5, 2023, signed a contract with the Special Operations Forces. On September 13, 2023, he was injured in the Kharkiv region.
